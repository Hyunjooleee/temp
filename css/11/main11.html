Every great mistake has a halfway moment, a split second when it can be recalled and perhaps remedied. True hope is swift, and flies with swallow's wings; Kings it makes gods, and meaner creatures kings. War may sometimes be a necessary evil. But no matter how necessary, it is always an evil, never a good. We will not learn how to live together in peace by killing each other's children. It is not worth an intelligent man's time to be in the majority. By definition, there are already enough people to do that. Grief is the agony of an instant, the indulgence of grief the blunder of a life.
